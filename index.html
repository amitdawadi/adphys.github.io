Welcome!

Welcome to my webpage! I am Amit Dawadi, a PhD student in physics at Clark University. I am working in the Nonlinear Physics Laboratory led by Prof. Arshad Kudrolli. My research focuses on understanding complex matter where structure, geometry, and mechanics lead to emergent behaviors, including crumpling phenomena, twisting filaments, and self-propulsion of ice blocks.
